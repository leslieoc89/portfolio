About Me

Strategist. Marketer. Builder.

I am a senior marketing professional with 15+ years in SaaS and technology, with deep expertise in product marketing, content strategy, and go-to-market execution. I started in enterprise sales, which means I understand the full revenue funnel and what it takes to move buyers from awareness to close.
